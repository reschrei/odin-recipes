Lasagna

Description

This Lasagna Recipe is easy to make with a creamy ricotta cheese mixture, savory meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.

You won't believe the flavor that's packed in this easy to cook meal!

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Spices
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Beef & Sausage
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella
  • Egg
  • Lasagna
  • Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • Pamesan

Steps

  1. Mix Ingredients
  2. Layer pasta in dish
  3. Place in oven for 4 hours at 250 degrees
  4. Feed to orange cat once cooled to approperate temp

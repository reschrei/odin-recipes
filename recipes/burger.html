Burger

Description

The all american burger as a great way to kick off a weekend at the beach or in the back yard.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Seasoning
  • Amercian Cheese (Optional)

Steps

  1. Form patties by hand to the size of a hockey puck
  2. Heat grill to Medium-High heat
  3. Cook on each side for 5 minutes for Medium-Well
  4. Add cheese to patty on the grill about 40 seconds before the second side is finished
